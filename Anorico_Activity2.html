General 1: Johannes Erwin Eugen Rommel

Johannes Erwin Eugen Rommel or known as Erwin Rommel, was a german field marshal during ww2 Popularly known as the Desert Fox, he served in the Wermacht of Nazi Germany, as well as serving in the Reichswehr of the Weimar Republic, and the Imperial Army of Germany. Rommel was injured in both World Wars (World War 1 and 2)

General 2: Fritz Erich Georg Eduard von Manstein

Fritz Erich Georg Eduard von Manstein or Erich von Manstein was a Field Marshal in the here of the Nazi Germany during World War 2. He was subsequently convicted of war crimes and sentenced to 18 years of imprisonment.

General 3: Graf Alfred von Schlieffen

Graf Alfred von Schlieffen or Alfred von Schlieffen, generally called Count Schlieffen, was a Field Marshal and strategist who served as a chief in the Imperial German General Staff from 1981 - 1906.
